About SaniSip
SaniSip allows easy, real-time monitoring of campus water quality using IoT-based sensors and cloud data — no login required.
How SaniSip Works
Sensors (TDS, pH, Turbidity) feed live readings to a campus database via Wi-Fi. SaniSip reads from the database and shows real-time safety status and sensor data right on your phone.
